Where I've Lived

I've always lived in suburban regions within 15-30 minutes of a major US city.

I enjoy being close enough to a city to take advantage of the work and entertainment opportunities, but far enough to be able to have a yard and some space to spread out.

Philadelphia, PA Area

The majority of my life was spent in New Jersey, about 15 minutes away from Philadelphia. I grew up, went to college, met my wife, and started following my favorite teams in this region.

Nashville, TN Area

I only spent a year in Nashville, but my wife and I loved the area and would have stayed longer if not for the combination of the dotcom bubble bursting and 9/11 tightening the job market in general.

Jacksonville, FL Area

My family and I have enjoyed the weather in sunny Florida for the past dozen years. We've had a lot of opportunities to enjoy outdoor activities and theme parks and it's where my kids have grown up.
